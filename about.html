A Muslim sports organization built on faith, forged through competition, and growing across New England.

Our Mission


TO FOSTER UNITY AND STRENGTHEN THE BONDS OF THE UMMAH THROUGH SPORT AND COMMUNITY.

RHM began as an inspired vision shared by three devoted individuals who saw a gap in our community — a place where Muslim athletes could compete, connect, and grow in faith together.

From those early conversations, Reuniting Hearts of Muslims emerged as a force for connection across New England. We organize leagues, tournaments, pickup games, and community events that bring the ummah together in a spirit of brotherhood and healthy competition.

Our mission is not simply to run sports leagues. It is to create a space where Muslim identity and athletic ambition are not in conflict — where you can show up fully as both a believer and a competitor, and where every game is also an act of community building.

We believe the deen calls us to strengthen our bonds, and we believe sport is one of the most powerful tools for doing exactly that.

New England's Largest

THE TOURNAMENT THAT STARTED A MOVEMENT.

What started as a humble gathering of brothers with a shared love of the game has grown into something historic. RHM now hosts New England's largest Muslim basketball tournament — an event that draws teams from across the region, uniting athletes in faith and competition under one roof.

Every year the tournament grows. Every year new friendships are forged, new memories made, and the ummah of New England grows stronger. This is not just a tournament — it is a statement that our community thrives, competes, and belongs.

The anniversary of RHM's first tournament marks more than a milestone on a calendar. It marks the moment our community decided to show up for itself — and has never stopped since.

What We Stand For

OUR VALUES

01
Faith
Everything we do is grounded in our deen. Faith is not a backdrop to our work — it is the foundation. We play, we organize, and we build community as Muslims first.
02
Unity
The ummah is one. Regardless of background, age, or skill level, every Muslim is welcome at RHM. Sport is a language that transcends all differences and builds lasting bonds.
03
Community
We are not just an organization — we are a community. We show up for each other on the court and off it. Every event is an opportunity to make the ummah stronger.
