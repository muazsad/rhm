FROM AN INSPIRED VISION TO NEW ENGLAND'S PREMIER MUSLIM SPORTS COMMUNITY.

RHM began as an inspired vision shared by three devoted individuals who saw a gap in our community — a place where Muslim athletes could compete, connect, and grow in faith together.


From those early conversations, Reuniting Hearts of Muslims emerged as a force for connection across New England. Our mission is to foster unity and strengthen the bonds of the ummah — one game, one tournament, one community at a time.


We believe sports are more than competition. They are a language of brotherhood, a proving ground for character, and a catalyst for the kind of community our deen calls us to build.

New England's Largest

THE PREMIER MUSLIM BASKETBALL TOURNAMENT IN NEW ENGLAND.

What started as a humble gathering of brothers with a shared love of the game has grown into something historic. RHM hosts New England's largest Muslim basketball tournament — an event that draws teams from across the region, uniting athletes in faith and competition under one roof.

Every year the tournament grows. Every year new friendships are forged, new memories made, and the ummah of New England grows stronger. This is not just a tournament — it is a statement that our community thrives, competes, and belongs.
